Chocolate cake

Home

A super-soft chocolate cake with a melt-in-your-mouth milk chocolate filling.

ingredients

Mass

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup(tea) of oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (tea) of sugar
  • 1 cup (tea) of chocolate powder or chocolate milk
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 cup (tea) of hot water

Coverage

  • 4 tablespoons of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup (tea) of sugar

Steps

  1. In a blender, beat the eggs, sugar, oil, chocolate powder and wheat flour.
  2. Pour the dough into a bowl and add the warm water and yeast, mixing well.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake in a preheated medium-high oven (200Â° C) for 40 minutes.
  4. Unmold while still hot.
  5. For a coverage:In a saucepan, heat all the ingredients until it boils.
  6. Pour it on top of the cake while it is still hot.
